Get Involved!

So in case you hadn't realised, we’re not exactly going for a traditional wedding… We’ve chosen not to have a gift list, in favour of a truly participatory wedding where you can give as you like - time, energy, skills or creativity.

This is a chance to let your hair down, not care about what anyone else thinks and have fun.

You can get involved pretty much anyway you want but below are some suggested areas in which you can volunteer… We’ll ask you for more details on the RSVP.

Activities

  • Campfire pyromaniac
  • Marshmallow King / Queen (anyone want toasted marshmallows over the bonfire?)
  • Outdoor games: Highland Games, Kube, Mölky, treasure hunt, hide and seek etc.
  • Indoor games - Board Games, Quiz etc.
  • Stargazing (anyone have their own telescope we could borrow?)
  • Walk in Daviot woods
  • Whisky or Gin Tasting
  • Taize
  • Slackline school
  • Circus skills
  • Shelter / den building
  • Zumba / Yoga / Tai Chi / Pilates
  • Programme Coordinator - co-ordinate all of the above!

Music and arts

  • Playing music
  • Organising a scratch band
  • Story-telling
  • Poetry
  • Spoken word
  • Stand up comedy
  • Talks
  • Dance
  • Writing
  • Exhibitions
  • Contributors Coordinator - co-ordinate all of the above!

Kids

We've got a whole load of kids coming to Kith and Kin and we want them to have as much fun as adults, whilst giving parents some space! Help us make this possible...

  • Bouncy Castle supervisors (there will be an adult bouncy castle too)
  • Face painting
  • Arts and crafts
  • Puppet shows
  • Fancy dress
  • Outdoor and indoor games (as above)
  • Kids Coordinator - co-ordinating all of the above and making sure everything is in place over the weekend

Food

  • Sourcing a churros van!
  • Liaising with on-site catering
  • Food Coordinator - making sure everyone is fed

Setup & Logistics

  • Site Manager
  • Site vibing (lights, signage, flowers, bunting, flags)
  • Transport (pick ups and drop offs)
  • Festival pack design and creation

Networks

Recommendations and introductions for any of the above!

You really want to give?

Oh, alright then… We’re not going to stop you if you’re THAT insistent. We'd love a contribution to our honey sun-moon-earth fund (ask Simon for more details). Alternatively - adlib and give us something to treasure.
