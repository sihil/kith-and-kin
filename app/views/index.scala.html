About Us

After 7 years of friendship, various meals, drinks, parties and several sailing holidays, romance was born. And what was the great catalyst that brought about this life changing spark? Homeland.

Christina was doing her Masters full time and working part time. Simon was being Simon - generous and caring and geeky. Christina’s one evening off a week (prior to Monday morning’s early start) was Sunday night - going to a little church community that Simon was also part of, then watching Homeland at Simon’s house (helped by the Tivo box for anytime watching).

And so, hanging out on Sunday evenings transpired into a drink… A drink (once Simon realised what was happening) transpired into a serious talk. A talk turned into more talk, a kiss, consultation on house buying, a romantic weekend in Wales, skiing in Austria, a sailing holiday in Turkey, getting roped into writing a bibilography for a dissertation, playing with nieces and nephews, stargazing and… a proposal.
